Accounts in chaos? Find a Bookkeeper here

Don’t waste time trawling online trying to find a reliable bookkeeper to run your accounts – find the best bookkeepers in the business right here. Every IAB member listed is qualified, professional and regulated with all the skills you need! Search by location, specific services such as wages and business tax, or by name. Once you’ve found who you are looking for, simply contact the bookkeeper directly to find out how they can help. Try it, you’ll be spoilt for choice!

If you have any questions relating to our professional members and the services they offer please view the Frequently Asked Questions below.

Details which appear on the Find a Bookkeeper section of the IAB website are kept secure and security procedures are in place on the website to significantly reduce the risk of any spam messages being sent.

If you notice any details which are incorrect on this page please let us know by contacting the compliance team: compliance@iab.org.uk or +44 (0)1732 897750.

Please note: The IAB does not permit anyone to contact its members using any form of bulk communication via the Find a Bookkeeper Section of the website.

Are IAB bookkeepers a member of a professional body?

All individuals listed on the Find a Bookkeeper page are professional members with the IAB. This means bookkeepers will be supervised under the Money Laundering Regulations 2007 and will be qualified and experienced in the work they do. Membership with the IAB is a sign of professional excellence and a sign to employers and businesses that the individual is a professional at the top of their field.

Are IAB bookkeepers qualified and are these qualifications recognised?

In order to gain membership with the IAB applicants do need to have an IAB level 2 qualification or higher.  We also have an exemption database system which allows individuals who have gained an equivalent qualification with another awarding body to enter into membership.  A number of awarding bodies on the system do have recognised qualifications and the IAB offers extensive financial qualifications – accredited by Ofqual, which is the non-ministerial government department that regulates qualifications, exams and tests in England and vocational qualifications in Northern Ireland.

Are IAB bookkeepers up to date with the latest information and legislation to assist their clients?

As members of the IAB they receive regular technical updates and access to a range of resources to assist them with various legislation and changes taking place.  IAB bookkeepers also have the opportunity to attend a number of seminars and training sessions throughout the year which often have speakers from HMRC, The Pensions Regulator and many more.

What experience do IAB bookkeepers have?

To find out more about the experience and services which IAB members are able to offer please scroll down to view the individual profiles.

How much can I expect to pay for the services of one of your bookkeepers?

We are unable to provide an exact answer regarding this as prices do vary depending on the type of work which you require to be carried out and also the region in which you work.  We usually suggest you try contacting several bookkeepers in your area to see how much they charge.
